# **Top Down**

## **How To Become a Turing Student**

```
1 Quit Old Job
  1.1 write resignation letter
    1.1.1 paper copy
      1.1.1.1 scribble thoughts on scrap paper
      1.1.1.2 transcribe into word processor
      1.1.1.3 print out paper copy
      1.1.1.4 sign document
        1.1.1.4.1 locate writing instrument
          1.1.1.4.1.1 determine ink color
          1.1.1.4.1.2 prepare instrument to write
        1.1.1.4.2 apply signature
    1.1.2 digital copy
  1.2 submit resignation letter
    1.2.1 digital copy
    1.2.1 paper copy
2 Determine New Career
  2.1 take extended well deserved hiatus
  2.2 soul search
    2.2.1 missed opportunities in life
    2.2.2 childhood dreams
    2.2.3 determine desired challenge level
3 Training Search
4 Select Training Method
5 Enroll In Training
6 Arrive for First Day
```
